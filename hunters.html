SquirrelMapper


Do you hunt or trap squirrels? We can use your samples.

  • Just a tiny clip of ear tissue is all we need to isolate DNA and better understand how coat color in squirrels evolves.

  • We can use samples from ANY eastern gray squirrel in the U.S. or Canada, regardless of their coat color (gray, black, or others).

  • Squirrels must be harvested legally. If you are a hunter, that means you need a valid hunting license and are following hunting regulations for squirrels in your state or province. We can also use samples from squirrels legally taken by licensed pest management professionals.

  • If you’re interested, fill out the form linked below, and we’ll send you simple instructions and basic materials (vials for samples, shipping container) needed to collect tissue and send it to us.
